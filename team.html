Current Members

Katherine Polasek
Katherine Polasek is a recent graduate from the University of Michigan Masters of Science in Information program. Her research focuses on censorship and information access.
Rachel Huang
Rachel Huang (she/hers) is a Senior Project Manager at ICPSR, managing research projects related to data curation and archiving and assisting researchers throughout the data archiving lifecycle in the social sciences, from initial deposit to public dissemination. She leads a team of Project Managers and manages a portfolio of federally and state-funded projects, drawing on experience in nonprofit administration and field research. Rachel holds a BA in Psychology from Michigan State University. She is a first-year graduate student at the University of Michigan's School of Information, specializing in Library, Archives, and Knowledge Environments.
